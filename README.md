  <h2 id="overview">📋 Project Overview</h2>
  <p style="max-width: 700px; text-align: center;">
    RentalCar provides a user-friendly interface for renting cars online. Explore the catalog, filter by your needs, and book your dream car in a few clicks. The project follows a professional design and uses a ready-made backend API from GoIT.
  </p>

  <br>

  <h2 id="features">✨ Features</h2>
  <div style="text-align: left; display: inline-block; min-width: 300px;">
    <p>🔹 <b>Smart Filtering:</b> Brand, price, and mileage (backend-driven).</p>
    <p>🔹 <b>Favorites System:</b> Save cars you love (LocalStorage persistence).</p>
    <p>🔹 <b>Pagination:</b> "Load More" button for smooth browsing.</p>
    <p>🔹 <b>State Management:</b> Powered by Zustand for global consistency.</p>
    <p>🔹 <b>Booking Form:</b> Integrated date picker and success notifications.</p>
  </div>

  <hr width="50%">

  <h2 id="tech-stack">🛠️ Tech Stack</h2>
  <p>
    <b>Frontend:</b> Next.js 15 (App Router), React, TypeScript, CSS Modules<br>
    <b>State & API:</b> Zustand, Axios<br>
    <b>Tools:</b> ESLint, Prettier, Vercel
  </p>

  <hr width="50%">

  <h2 id="pages">📂 Pages Structure</h2>
  <div style="text-align: left; display: inline-block;">
    <p>🏠 <code>/</code> — <b>Home:</b> Hero banner with CTA.</p>
    <p>🏎️ <code>/catalog</code> — <b>Catalog:</b> Full car list with filters and pagination.</p>
    <p>🔍 <code>/catalog/:id</code> — <b>Details:</b> Full specs and rental booking form.</p>
  </div>

  <h2 id="setup">🚀 Installation & Setup</h2>
  <div style="text-align: left; background: #2d2d2d; color: #ccc; padding: 20px; border-radius: 8px; font-family: monospace; max-width: 500px;">
    <p>1. Clone the repository:</p>
    <code style="color: #fff;">git clone https://github.com/IrynaYermak/car-rental</code><br>
    <code style="color: #fff;">cd car-rental</code>
    
    <p>2. Install dependencies:</p>
    <code style="color: #fff;">npm install</code>
